Office of Internal Auditor General (OIAG)


The OIAG is setup to perform its functions under Section 116 of the Public Finance Management, (Act 2016). The Office is required to provide assurance and advisory services and promote the practice of internal auditing in the public service. The OIAG works with the Paymaster General and other relevant stakeholders to promote the practice of internal audit and enhance the work of the internal auditors for quality internal audit service in the public sector. This has contributed to transparency, accountability and safeguarding of public sector resources.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS


In the past six months, the Office has chalked the following successes;
Training & Capacity Building
In pursuance of its mandate, the Office trained internal auditors from Public Institutions both Unguja and Pemba in: 1. Internal Audit Manual 2. Quality Assurance Manual 3. Operational Plan 4. TRA online Payment System 5. Government Audit Recommendation Implementation Information Tracking System (GARI – ITS)
• Evaluation of Capacity Needs Assessment
In line with Section 116(2) (i) of Act 2016, the Office supervises the training to internal auditors, hence it undertook evaluation of capacity need assessment to internal auditors so as to determine the knowledge gap. Thus, evaluation will help the Office to ascertain the effectiveness and efficiency of internal audit works. Such evaluation assessed the capabilities of internal auditors in areas of priority for the Public Service.
• Establishment of Zanzibar Internal Auditors System (ZIAS)
As a part of reforming the management of internal auditors throughout the Public Institutions, the Office has designed the system capable of administering the internal auditors’ affairs including registration, rotation and training of internal auditors as per Section 116(2) (i) of Act 2016 instructed. This will ensure smooth operation in dealing with all relevant matters pertaining to internal auditors.

CONCLUSION

The Internal Auditor General takes this opportunity to express her appreciation and gratitude to stakeholders for their continued support to the Office. This new website is expected to be very interactive and to bridge the communication gap between the Office and its stakeholders.
